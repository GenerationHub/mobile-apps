Frequently asked questions

Is my data private?

Yes. Your streak, check-ins, and notes are stored locally on your device. Reclaim requires no account and never sends your recovery data to our servers.

What does Reclaim Pro include?

Reclaim Pro is a one-time purchase (no subscription). It unlocks the full panic-button toolkit, the complete recovery timeline, your discipline rating and full stats, and backup & restore.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, go to the “You” tab, and tap Restore Purchase. Make sure you’re signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Reclaim Pro.

I relapsed — did I lose everything?

No. When you reset your streak, your best streak, total clean days, and XP are all kept. There’s no shame here — just get back up and keep going.

How does the panic button work?

When an urge hits, tap “I’m having an urge.” Reclaim guides you through a breathing exercise and a short timer to ride the wave until it passes — most urges peak and fade within a few minutes.

Is Reclaim medical treatment?

No. Reclaim is a wellness and self-improvement tool, not medical advice. If you’re struggling, please reach out to a qualified professional.
