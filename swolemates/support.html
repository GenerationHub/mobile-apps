FAQ

How do I add my partner?

Create a crew, then tap "Invite — share code" and send them the code. They open SwoleMates, choose "My partner sent me a code," and enter it.

Do we both need iPhones?

Yes — SwoleMates syncs over iCloud, so each member needs an iPhone signed into iCloud.

What goals can a crew track?

Workouts, steps, runs, cycling, healthy eating, active minutes, or a custom goal — set when you create the crew.

How do wagers work?

Set a fun stake (e.g. "loser buys coffee"). Each week, whoever misses their goal while the other hits owes one reward. The person owed can clear the balance.

How do I report or block someone?

Tap the "…" menu on any photo in the Feed to report it or block the person. Reported content is reviewed and removed within 24 hours.

How do I leave a crew?

Go to the You tab, long-press a crew, and choose Leave.

What's in SwoleMates Pro?

A one-time purchase that unlocks wagers, full history, crew leaderboards, the home-screen widget, and unlimited crews.
