Frequently asked questions

Is my financial data private?

Yes. Your transactions, accounts, and envelopes are stored locally on your device. Stash never links to your bank and never sends your financial data to our servers.

What does Stash Pro include?

Stash Pro is a one-time purchase (no subscription). It unlocks unlimited transactions, unlimited accounts and envelopes, insights & spending charts, and backup & restore.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, go to the upgrade screen, and tap Restore Purchase. Make sure you’re signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy Stash Pro.

I switched phones — how do I keep my data?

Stash data lives on your device and in your iCloud backup. Restoring your new iPhone from your iCloud backup brings your Stash data with it. Your Pro purchase restores via the Apple ID you bought it with.

How do I delete my data?

Deleting the app removes all locally stored Stash data from your device.
